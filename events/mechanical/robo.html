19th and 20th Feb. 2014

Pick and Place

. . . . lets play with alphabets.

Introduction

This February witness the most brutal of all the Robotics competitions when two combat Robots go head to head to earn the ultimate glory!!! Yes, a typical pick and place competition. However, I should state a difference by underlining the world REAL-TIME. Design a robot that has the right mix of control, aggression and speed and capable of doing given task in minimum time.

Details

Game Play :

Participants need to build a bot with arm mechanism which can lift differently shaped objects and place them at different place. The ultimate aim is to form given words with provided alphabets blocks.

Dimensions: :

  • 1. Height of Robo - 25 cm max.
  • 2. Weight of Robo - max.7 kg.
  • 3. Size of ball - 3 cm diameter.
  • 4. Two platforms at the height of 35 cm.
  • 5. Square blocks of alphabets having maximum weight 100 gm and dimensions of 4*4*4cm and 3*3*3 cm

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. The team will be required to pick and place objects using a robotic arm.
  • 2. Scoring will be based on number of words formed, accuracy of the placement and time taken by the team to complete the task.
  • 3. The robot is expected to start from a designated "home" position, trace the track, pick the blocks of alphabets from the given position, return to the “home” position through same track and form the words by placing the blocks on the platform.
  • 4. The robot should carry only one block at a time.
  • 5. Each team will be supplied power strip.
  • 6. The footprint will be provided at the competition.
  • 7. The decisions of the judges are final and binding
  • 8. In the second round, with square blocks there will be balls of maximum weight 100 gm of 3 cm diameter also that you have to drop into sack.

Certification Policy

All the students will be given participation certificate.
Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence.

Team Details

A team can have a maximum of 3 members.
Team members can be from different colleges or years.

Entry Fees

Rs. 200/-per team at registration.( maximum 3 members in team)
Rs. 100/- after shortlisting.
