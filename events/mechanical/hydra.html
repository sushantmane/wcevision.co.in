Hydraulic Weight Lifter

. . . . Make your own Lifting Mechanism.

Introduction

Make a manually controlled machine, which can lift maximum weight using hydraulic mechanism only.

Details

Machine Specification :

  • 1. Base of machine should fit into a box of dimension 50 cm X 50 cm at the time of start. There is no restriction on the height of the machine. The extensions used to control the machine are excluded while measuring the machine dimensions.
  • 2. A lifting pan must be there in the machine on which weights have to be placed.
  • 3. Machine can expand or compress during the run.
  • 4. Use of batteries or any other source of stored energy is prohibited.
  • 5. Use of any type of electrical motors is prohibited.
  • 6. Use of springs is prohibited.
  • 7. Use of ready made assembly is not allowed.

Materials allowed :

  • Syringes (or any other cylinder-piston arrangement), tubes, wood, metallic frames, metallic pins, rubber bands, plastic, water, oil , ready-made tyres.

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. Machine should lift the weight minimum by 5 cm from original position of Lifting Pan.
  • 2. Starting weight is 5 kg.
  • 3. Weights will be in the multiples of 500 grams.
  • 4. Each participating team will get 3 trials. In each trial team will have to decide the weight to be placed on the lifting pan.
  • 5. The machine must lift weight within 30 seconds.
  • 6. The system should remain stable in its lifted state for at least another 30 seconds after the lifting ends.
  • 7. For controlling of machine only hand operation is allowed.

Certification Policy

1.All the teams which lift the weight minimum by 5 cm will get Certificate of Participation.

2. Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence.

Best Innovation Model

Apart from lifting mechanism, participating team will allowed to show extra innovative mechanism like damping, motion with cylinder piston arrangement etc. This will be consider for "Excellence certificate in innovative model designing". it is not compulsory.

Team Details

1.A team can have a maximum of 3 members.
2.Team members can be from different colleges or years.

Entry Fees

Rs. 200/- (per team)(considering 2 members per team)
Rs. 300/- (per team)(considering 3 members per team)
