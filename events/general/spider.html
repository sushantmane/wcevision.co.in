19th and 20th Feb. 2014

Spider Instinct

. . . . on the spot paper presentation competition.

Introduction

Each of us being an engineer has an inherent natural instinct of presenting our ideas to the world. This event gives an opportunity to test extremities of your instinct by presenting an extempore.

Details

Novice : FE, SE, FY, SY (C/ C++)
Expert : TE, BE, TY and onwards (No Language barrier - C/C++/JAVA/Python)

Round 1 - Screening Test (From 09.00AM to 10.00AM):

  • A screening test will be based on General Aptitude , Quantitative Aptitude , Verbal â€“ Non Verbal, Vocabulary and general knowledge questions.
  • The test will be of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ).

Round 2-Surprise Round (From 12.00PM to 5.00PM):
(1 hr 30 min preparation + 3 hr 30 min presentation & judging)
This is final round where teams will be provided with INTERNET facility and will be given a general topic.
The teams need to prepare a Powerpoint Presentation (ppt) of 5- 6 minutes within given time on given subject.

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. Participants will be provided with the necessary softwares and equipments by the event coordinators.
  • 2. Students must bring their valid college identity cards while coming to event.
  • 3. Any team that is not present at the time specified will be disqualified immediately.
  • 4. Rules may be changed without prior intimation.
  • 5. Participants are requested to check the website regularly for updates.

Certification Policy

Shortlisted students from 1st round will be given participation certificate.
Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence.

Team Details

A team can have a maximum of 2 members.
Team members can be from different colleges or years.

Entry Fees

For first round- RS 50/-
For Second round- RS 150/-
