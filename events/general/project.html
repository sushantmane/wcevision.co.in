19th and 20th Feb. 2014

PROJECT PRESENTATION

. . . . A project presentation competition.

Introduction

Cultivating, nurturing an idea and eventually striving hard to realize it is the work centre of an engineer. So plough the idea and get it out in the market to test its feasibility and utility so that it may be utilized for human welfare.

Details

Novice Group:Students of F.E., S.E., F.Y. & S.Y.
Expert Group:Students of T.E., B.E., M.E. & T.Y.

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. Projects will be considered under 4 groups:-
    Electronics & Electrical
    Mechanical Engineering
    CSE & IT
    General (new group for general projects)
  • 2. Project report is to be submitted with 1 soft copy with maximum 15 pages on A4 size paper only, font size 12 Times New Roman & additional abstract with maximum 100 words with participants name & college name.
  • 3. Bring one hard copy at the time of presentation.
  • 4. Participant should be convey their requirement 5 days prior to event.

Presentation Policy

  • 1. Working model/software.
  • 2. Simulation and working video.
  • 3. Bring one hard copy of project report.
  • 4. Time limit: max.15 min.
    Introduction : 3 min.
    Demo and explanation : 7 min.
    Future scope : 1 min.
    Conclusion : 1 min.
    Question-answer : 3 min.

Certification Policy

All the students will be given participation certificate.
Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence.

Team Details

A team can have a maximum of 3 members.
Team members may be from different colleges. However one person cannot be a part of multiple teams for the same event.

Entry Fees

Rs. 300 per team(for team of 3 members) and 100 Rs. extra for any extra member.
