19th and 20th Feb. 2014

Awakens

. . . . Let The Revolution Begin.

Introduction

In today's world, where technologies have reached sky-high, engineering is at its acme and science has grown by leaps and bounds due to the relentless human efforts, the one thing that slips through is about the society and the surrounding environment.

It's our onus to give back to the society, because we have gained a lot and prospered by using the resources available in it. Let's spare some time and give a thought to the reigning issues of our society.

So here, at VISION 2014 we present before you, ' THE AWAKENS ' , an opportunity for the young minds to use their beliefs as solid as rocks, values stronger than steel and concern which is inherently humane to find solutions and pave a path for making the world a better place to live in.

Get ready for the awakening.

Details

Rounds Information :

  • 1st Round:- Report(hard copy) + Poster
  • 2nd Round:- Presentation and Documentary (8+2 min)
  • 3rd Round:- Surprise Round

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. At the time of registration participant teams should come with a topic on social issue. The choice of topic depends solely on the participants wish and it will be on first come first serve basis.
  • 2. The poster may be done on A4 size with coral draw, Photoshop, paint, any other development software or handmade. It may contain real time photos, cartoons, slogans, baselines etc.
  • 3. Maximum no. entries allotted: 25.
  • 4. Students must bring their valid college identity cards while coming to event.
  • 5. All teams and their respective members should reach the event place in time. Tardiness may be considered as grounds for disqualification.
  • 6. All teams must maintain decorum at all times. Organizers may disqualify any team that behaves inappropriately.
  • 7. In case of disputes, the decision of event head and judges will be final and binding to all.
  • 8. The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website / forum.

Certification Policy

All the students will be given participation certificate.
Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence.

Team Details

A team can have a maximum of 4 members.
Team members may be from different colleges. However one person cannot be a part of multiple teams for the same event.

Entry Fees

- Rs. 200/- per team.
- Rs. 100/- After Short listing .
