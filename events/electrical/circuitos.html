19th and 20th Feb. 2014

Circuitos

. . . . A circuit designing competition.

Introduction

In the era of compact globalization it is essential to 'nanofy' each and every system. The control is the most important aspect of development. So engineers take the challenge of circuit designing and improvise your skills in electronics.

Details

Two categories :

  • 1. Novice: FY and SY(diploma), FE and SE
  • 2. Expert: TY(diploma), TE and BE.

Three Rounds :

3 rounds for both categories :

  • 1. Technical Aptitude test (on basic electronics and components ; difficulty level will differ for novice and expert category).
  • 2. Surprise round.
  • 3. Circuit mounting (actual building of circuit based on problem statement given by us).

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. Circuit should be built from provided components only, no extra components should be used.
  • 2. Participants should bring their own multimeter and minimum one breadboard if possible.
  • 3. Logic used, Proper working of circuits and time taken to design will decide your points.
  • 4. In case of disputes, the decision of event head and judges will be final and binding to all.

Certification Policy

All the students will be given participation certificate.
Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence.

Team Details

A team can have a maximum of 2 members.
Team members can be from different colleges or years.

Entry Fees

Rs. 50/- per team
Rs. 150 (After Shortlisting) Max. 2 participants
