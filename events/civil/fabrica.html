19th and 20th Feb. 2014

Fabrica

. . . . Construct your own structure.

Introduction

Build a T section using pop-sickle sticks as per the given specifications.

Details

The T section consists of two parts- Web and Flange.(Please refer the figure)

WEB :

  • 1. The height of the Web must be of 300 mm below the Flange.
  • 2. The cross section of the Web must be 80mm x 80mm.

FLANGE :

  • 1. Load will be attached to both ends of the section.
  • 2. The length of the flange must be of 250 mm on each side of the centre of the web.
  • 3. Total length of the flange must be 500 mm.
  • 4. Angle of inclination of flange must be 90 s° with respect to horizontal. (Please refer the figure).
  • 5. The width of the flange must be 80 mm.
  • 6. The depth of the flange may not exceed 80 mm.
  • 7. Participants will have to make an arrangement for hook at 20mm from each end of flange.

Materials to be used: :

  • 1. Standard pop sickle sticks / ice-cream sticks of maximum 12 cm in length, maximum 1.5 cm in width and maximum 4 mm thick.
  • 2. Only Adhesives (Synthetic white adhesive or single component cyano-acrylate adhesive) can be used to build the structure. (Adhesives such as M-Seal® are not allowed

Building Rules :

  • 1. The pop-sickle sticks can be trimmed and used in smaller pieces.
  • 2. Pop-sickle sticks can be stacked to make stronger elements.
  • 3. The structure of the section must be made entirely of these sticks and adhesives.
  • 4. The structure may not be painted, sprayed or dipped in any coating material. All glue should be removed from surfaces that are not bonded together.

Testing :

  • 1. Load should be attached on the ends of the flange.
  • 2. The load will be decided by the participant & the number of chances will not be more than 3.
  • (Max. load will be 15 kg on each sides of T section).
  • 3. Structure must carry the load for at least 30 seconds otherwise previous load will be considered for calculation.
  • 4. The hook for loading and metal base will be provided by organizer. (Please refer the figure)

Scoring :

  • Score = (Weight bear by structure /self weight of structure)

Rule and Regulations

  • 1. Shape: Structure could be of absolutely any shape satisfying the above constraints.
  • 2. Once the structure is weighed, you are not allowed to modify your structure in any way.
  • 3. If the structure fails to satisfy any of the above constraints then it will be summarily rejected.
  • 4. Any team that is not ready at the time specified will be disqualified immediately.
  • 5. Judge's decision shall be final and binding on all.
  • 6. The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website / forum.

Certification Policy

  • 1. All the participating teams will get Certificate of Participation.
  • 2. Top two teams will be awarded the Certificate of Excellence

Team Details

1. A team can have a maximum of 2 members.
2. Team members can be from different colleges or years.

Entry Fees

- Rs. 200/-per team.
