19th and 20th Feb. 2014

Ship Building

. . . . a ship model making event.

Introduction

Jack Sparrow is back with new exciting adventures. After fortifying back the ship, a new thrilling challenge has sprung up testing his fate. So assist him in his fight back for survival and conquering the oceans, design a buoyant body which will be the key of his future.

Theme :

Jack Sparrow reached his destination with treasure in his ship, The BLACK PEARL. As his dream was full of lust and greed, Sparrow now is trying to free himself from an old debt with the villainous Davy Jones by finding the Dead Mans Chest which he can use to command Jones to do his bidding. But then, he finds himself against the vicious, dreaded and notorious Kraken who has the ability of destroying the entire world.

In the previous voyage, he faced a hazy storm, which completely demolished the ship. And now Sparrow with his blood boiling, have eventually decided to take the revenge against the Kraken.

Now, he is planning launch a fierce attack on that dreaded beast. To launch such an attack, he will have to build the ship which will carry as much and armaments and weapons as possible.

So, help him out to build the strong and durable ship which will survive storms and stand over the sea establishing his supremacy over the oceans and abolish the Kraken.

Details

Statement :

  • Construct a buoyant structure within given constraints which must float on water with maximum load with safety & can carry distance with load in minimum time.

Specifications :

  • 1. Max. Dimension of ship: l*b*h= 40cm*25cm*20cm.
  • 2. The ship should bear minimum load of 3kg.
  • 3. Pulling mechanism will be made by us. You have to make appropriate arrangement for attaching a string to the ship.
  • 4. Loads on pulling mechanism will be in multiples of 0.5 kg.
  • 5. Following figure shows the arrangement of pulley, load, ship etc.

Testing :

  • 1. There will be two attempts of loading on ship.
  • 2. For each attempt of loading ship, there will be two attempts of loading on pulley
  • 3. Loads on ship and pulley will be decided by participants.
  • 4. Firstly load will applied on ship only and ship will be allowed to stabilize for 10 sec.
  • 5. Then loading on pulley will be done and time for traveling distance of 10 feet of loaded ship is recorded.
  • 6. For each travel, there will be constant load on pulley.
  • 7. Loading on ship and pulley will be done in increasing order.
  • 8. For every attempt, loading will be done at start point only.
  • 9. In case if the ship submerges during pulling or loading on ship due to any reason, that attempt will not be considered.
  • 10. Last valid score will be considered for final score.
  • 11. The ship should not leak during any attempt.

Scoring :

  • Scoring will be based on 4 parameters
  • 1. Load (in kg) on ship as well as on pulley.
  • 2. Change in dimension
  • 3. Time taken for travelling 10ft.
  • 4. Judge

Formula for Score :

  • Score=(( 10*Load on ship / Load on pulley)+( 100 / time for travel in sec)-( 2.5*Δb+5*Δl+4*Δh ))
  • Where
    • Δb=change in breadth
    • Δl=change in length
    • Δh=change in height

Note :

  • 1. Changes will be considered in set of 5mm.
  • 2. For e.g. 5mm change in length, ΔL=1 and for 12 mm change in length Δ L=3.
